// what it does ─────────────────────────

pick a film stock. see it in the viewfinder. shoot. what you see is what you get. portra warmth, delta grain, cinestill halation — all happening in real time before you press the shutter.

// features ──────────────────────────────
┌─ 01
manual camera

full control over shutter speed, iso, white balance, and exposure compensation. shoot like you would with a film body.

┌─ 02
live film preview

see the film stock look in real time through the viewfinder. no guessing. what you see is what you get.

┌─ 03
film stock simulations

30+ stocks built from real film data. color negative, slide, cinema, black & white. the actual curves, color shifts, and highlight rolloff.

┌─ 04
full exif metadata

shutter speed, iso, aperture, focal length, lens info, gps, color space — saved with every shot. tap to see everything.

┌─ 05
photo library

browse your photos, check metadata, select and share. swipe through shots with full exposure details.

┌─ 06
on-device. always.

no cloud. no accounts. no uploads. every photo, every edit, every simulation runs on your phone. your photos stay yours.
