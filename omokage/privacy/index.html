Name

omokage — privacy policy

Last Updated

march 26, 2026

Description

Omokage is developed by Thomas Yokota (miraisoft). this policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Omokage does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third parties. there are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no tracking pixels, nothing.

Camera Access

Omokage uses the camera for the live viewfinder and photo capture. all frames are processed entirely on-device. camera data is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

Photo Library Access

Omokage accesses your photo library to save captured photos and to import photos for editing. photos are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded or transmitted.

On-Device Storage

the following data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • photos — captured images and edited exports
  • capture metadata — EV, ISO, shutter speed, aperture, color temperature, metering mode
  • editing projects — film stock selections, adjustment parameters, edit history
  • app preferences — settings and saved configurations

you can delete this data at any time within the app or by removing the app.

Analytics & Tracking

Omokage contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Subscriptions

subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. miraisoft does not process or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

the app does not integrate with any third-party services. there is no cloud sync, no external APIs, no data leaving your device.

Children's Privacy

the app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

updates to this policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

questions? reach me at hello@miraisoft.io.
