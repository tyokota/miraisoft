STOCKROOM(1) PRIVACY POLICY STOCKROOM(1)
Name

stockroom — privacy policy

Last Updated

march 8, 2026

Description

StockRoom is developed by Thomas Yokota. this policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

StockRoom does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third parties.

Photo Library

StockRoom accesses your photo library only to apply film simulations to photos you select. photos are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored outside your device.

processed images are saved back to your photo library if you choose to save them.

On-Device Storage

the following data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • preferences — app settings and saved adjustments

you can delete this data at any time by removing the app.

Analytics & Tracking

StockRoom contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

In-App Purchases

purchases are handled entirely by Apple. the developer does not receive or store payment information.

Third-Party Services

the app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

the app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

updates to this policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
