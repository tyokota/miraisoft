Name

mirai software — support

Synopsis

this page covers support for all mirai software apps, including SpotMeter and StockRoom.

Contact

for questions, bug reports, or feature requests, email hello@miraisoft.io.

i read every message and typically respond within a day or two.

Troubleshooting
  • make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store
  • try closing and reopening the app
  • restart your device if the issue persists
  • if the camera or photo library isn't working, check Settings > Privacy for permissions
Bug Reports

when reporting a bug, please include:

  • which app and version you're using
  • your device model and iOS version
  • what happened vs. what you expected
  • steps to reproduce, if possible
Privacy

all mirai software apps are private by design. no tracking, no analytics, no ads. your data stays on your device.

  • SpotMeter privacy policy
  • StockRoom privacy policy
