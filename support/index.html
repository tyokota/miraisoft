miraisoft — support

Synopsis

this page covers support for Omokage and all miraisoft apps.

Contact

for questions, bug reports, or feature requests, email hello@miraisoft.io.

i read every message and typically respond within a day or two.

Troubleshooting
  • make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store
  • try closing and reopening the app
  • restart your device if the issue persists
  • if the camera isn't working, check Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera and make sure Omokage has access
  • if you can't save or import photos, check Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos and make sure Omokage has access
Subscriptions

Omokage Pro is managed through the App Store. to manage or cancel your subscription:

  • open Settings on your iPhone
  • tap your name at the top, then Subscriptions
  • find Omokage and tap to manage or cancel
  • subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period

miraisoft does not have access to your payment information. all billing questions should go through Apple Support.

Bug Reports

when reporting a bug, please include:

  • which app and version you're using
  • your device model and iOS version
  • what happened vs. what you expected
  • steps to reproduce, if possible
Privacy

all miraisoft apps are private by design. no tracking, no analytics, no ads. your data stays on your device.
