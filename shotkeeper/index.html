// what it does ─────────────────────────

shotkeeper replaces your light meter app and your film simulation editor with one tool that connects them. meter the scene, shoot the frame, then develop your look — all without leaving the app.

// features ──────────────────────────────
┌─ 01
manual camera

full control over shutter speed, iso, white balance, and exposure compensation. shoot like you would with a film body.

┌─ 02
scene intelligence hud

dynamic range, color temperature, histogram — all live in the viewfinder. see what the scene is doing before you press the shutter.

┌─ 03
film stock simulations

37 stocks trained on real film scans. not filters — actual film character. portra warmth, tri-x grain, velvia punch. the camera recommends stocks based on what it sees.

┌─ 04
full photo editor

exposure, contrast, tone curve, saturation, hsl, split toning, grain, halation, bloom. everything you need to finish the shot without leaving the app.

┌─ 05
scene-aware suggestions

shotkeeper analyzes the scene — light, color, contrast — and recommends film stocks that match. you pick, it develops.

┌─ 06
on-device. always.

no cloud. no accounts. no uploads. every photo, every edit, every simulation runs on your phone. your photos stay yours.
