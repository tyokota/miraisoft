Service Description

ShotKeeper is a camera and photo editing app for iOS developed by Thomas Yokota (miraisoft). the app provides a manual camera, light metering tools, film stock simulations, and photo editing capabilities. all processing happens on your device.

Subscriptions

ShotKeeper offers a free camera with optional paid subscription ("ShotKeeper Pro") that unlocks film stock simulations and advanced editing tools.

  • subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID account via the App Store
  • subscriptions auto-renew unless you turn off auto-renewal at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period
  • you can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • pricing: $4.99/month or $29.99/year
  • free trial period, if offered, converts to a paid subscription unless cancelled before it ends

miraisoft does not process payments directly. all billing is handled by Apple under their standard EULA.
