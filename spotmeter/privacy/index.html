SPOTMETER(1) PRIVACY POLICY SPOTMETER(1)
Name

spotmeter — privacy policy

Last Updated

february 21, 2026

Description

SpotMeter is developed by Thomas Yokota. this policy describes how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

SpotMeter does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third parties.

On-Device Storage

the following data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it:

  • journal entries — metering readings, exposure settings, and notes
  • reference photos — images captured alongside metering data
  • voice memos — audio recordings attached to journal entries
  • location data — coordinates saved with journal entries, if you grant permission
  • preferences — settings, film stocks, and calibration values

you can delete this data at any time within the app or by removing the app.

Camera

the camera is used solely to meter light in real time. frames are processed on-device and are never stored, uploaded, or transmitted.

Analytics & Tracking

SpotMeter contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

In-App Purchases

purchases are handled entirely by Apple. the developer does not receive or store payment information.

Third-Party Services

the app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

the app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

updates to this policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
